About Us

“Ecology is our aim, and designing spaces is our objective.”

Ours is a Design and Environmental Planning firm working on various Landscape and Architectural projects. The range of projects include Landscape planning projects, preparation of Environmental Impact Assessment reports, preparation of Landscape management plans along with Landscape development proposals, Site planning projects and regular Architectural projects.

‘ECOSPACE’ has a multidisciplinary approach by involving Geologist, Hydrologist & Botanist in our projects. Ecospace has experience of working in different aspects of environmental activities with various governmental and non-governmental organizations, providing ecological study and restoration services.

The team includes professional Architects, Landscape Architects, Urban planners, a specialist in botany and ecology among other technical consultants of repute from Pune.
